We hope this is the beginning of something very special. Below are some milestones that will allow us to continue building this project.
25% - DAO Establishment
A DAO will be established for community sustainability. Holding an ProBitSnowMan will give you the right to vote on the future utility of ProBitSnowMan and give the community a chance to take the project wherever they desire.
50% - Community Wallet

40% of all mint sales will go into a community wallet. Where we will host both MATIC and NFT giveaways.

75% - Stake and earn Matic
Staking will be implemented. Hold your ProBitSnowMan to passively yield $Matic - which is used as official ProBitSnowMan token! Each ProBitSnowMan held earns 5 MATIC per month!
